Bangladesh national flag

Bangladesh, as an independent nation, has its own national flag. It is rectangular in shape, with a length-to-width ratio of 10:6. The flag features a prominent red circle at its center. The color green on our flag represents youthfulness, while the color red symbolizes the rising sun.

Pohela boisak

Pohela Boishakh, the first day of the Bengali New Year, is a joyous occasion celebrated with enthusiasm across Bangladesh and the state of West Bengal in India. It marks the beginning of the Bengali calendar year and is a time for new beginnings, cultural festivities, and family reunions. People wear new clothes, visit Boishakhi fairs, and enjoy traditional meal
